Real answers to the questions most people are afraid to ask before their first Sunday.

You'll be greeted at the entrance by our welcome team. No pressure, no spotlight moments — just a genuine hello. Find a seat, take in the worship, hear the Word preached straight. After the service, feel free to stay and meet people or head home. Either is perfectly fine.

Our Sunday gatherings are Spirit-led, Scripture-centered, and usually run about 90 minutes. Expect contemporary worship music, genuine prayer, and an expository message that goes deep into the Word — not a motivational talk. Malayalam service follows at 12:30 PM, honouring our Indian heritage.

Come as you are. Casual, smart-casual, traditional — whatever you're comfortable in. We care far more about you being in the room than what you have on.

We're in Hackensack, NJ. Sunday English worship is at 10:00 AM, Malayalam service at 12:30 PM. Wednesday Bible Study at 7:30 PM and Friday Youth Fellowship at 7:00 PM. Get directions above.

The full Sunday service streams live on our YouTube channel at 10:00 AM. The message is also available on demand the same day via YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Podcasts. Know that we see you and pray for you, even through a screen.

Children's Ministry — Formation Kids — runs during the 10:00 AM English service for all ages. Our servant leaders create safe, age-appropriate environments where kids genuinely encounter Jesus. Check in at the Kids Ministry desk in the main foyer when you arrive.

Yes. Wednesday Bible Study at 7:30 PM is a verse-by-verse deep dive — currently working through Romans. It's not a light devotional; it's discipleship that costs something. Come ready to open your Bible.

Small groups are where real community happens between Sundays — a smaller circle doing life together, studying Scripture, praying, and holding each other accountable. Ask any of our team on Sunday or reach out through our Connect page to find a group near you.
