Our Mission

To glorify God through Word, worship, and community

ICANJ exists to proclaim the gospel, make disciples, and serve the city of Hackensack — welcoming every person into a life-transforming relationship with Jesus Christ.

Who We Are

We are a multigenerational Indian-American church that honours the richness of our heritage while embracing our calling to be a light in New Jersey. Founded over two decades ago, ICANJ has grown into a vibrant family spanning all ages and backgrounds.

Doctrine

What We Believe

I
Scripture

The Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God — the supreme authority for faith, doctrine, and all of life.

II
The Trinity

One God, eternally existing in three co-equal persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

III
Jesus Christ

Fully God and fully man — born of a virgin, lived without sin, died in our place, and rose bodily from the dead.

IV
Salvation

By grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone. Not earned by works, but freely given by God.

V
The Holy Spirit

The Spirit indwells every believer, produces the fruit described in Scripture, and empowers the Church for mission.

VI
The Church

The body of Christ on earth — called to worship, discipleship, fellowship, ministry, and proclamation to all nations.
