Our Purpose

Our Mission

India Christian Assembly of New Jersey exists to glorify God through Spirit-filled worship, expository preaching, and compassionate community — welcoming all people into a life-changing relationship with Jesus Christ.

We are a multigenerational, multicultural church that honours our Indian heritage while embracing our call to be a light in New Jersey and the nations.

Doctrine

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the entire Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God and the supreme authority for faith and life.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — co-equal and co-eternal.

Jesus Christ

We believe in the full divinity and humanity of Jesus, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, and bodily resurrection.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone — not by works, but as a free gift from God.

The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit indwells every believer, empowers the Church, and produces the fruit and gifts described in Scripture.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ on earth — called to worship, discipleship, fellowship, ministry, and mission.

Our History

Our Story

1998
Foundations Laid

A small group of Indian Christian families in Hackensack began gathering in homes for worship and prayer, planting the seed of what would become ICANJ.

2003
First Permanent Home

ICANJ was formally established and moved into its first dedicated meeting space in northern New Jersey, marking a new chapter of growth.

2010
Growth & Expansion

Membership grew substantially. Dedicated children's and youth ministries were launched to serve every generation with intentional discipleship.

2024
Sermons Go Online

Weekly Bible study teaching became available on Spotify and Apple Podcasts, extending our reach well beyond the walls of our Hackensack campus.
